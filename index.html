Understanding Your Annual OB-GYN Exam

What Brownsville Women Should Expect

At Clinica Santa Maria, we’re dedicated to supporting women’s health in every stage of life. Your annual OB-GYN exam is a cornerstone of preventive care. Here’s what you can expect when you visit our Brownsville, TX clinic:

  • Pelvic and breast exams
  • Pap smears and HPV testing
  • Contraceptive counseling
  • Menstrual health and menopause guidance

We tailor every visit to your unique needs, offering compassionate, bilingual care in a comfortable setting. Our team is here to listen, educate, and support you at every step.
